SAXOPHONE LESSONS

The styles I specialise in teaching are Jazz, Swing, Funk, Pop (I am happy to accommodate other genres as well)

I believe learning music should be an enjoyable experience, and the way I teach is suited to the student's wishes and needs. I like to mix practical learning with music theory so pupils understand more about music generally.

Lessons available for children and adults, for beginners and more advanced students.

I charge 25/30/35 Euros per hour depending on your income and what you can afford. And this is done on a trust basis. I usually teach from my place in Neukoelln.

25€. For young guitarists.
35€. For adults, and the advancing guitarist
